About Skillet

Skillet is an American Christian rock band, lead by John Cooper.
It was formed in 1996, in Memphis, Tennessee, U.S.

Genres:

  • Christian Rock
  • Christian Metal
  • Hard Rock
  • Alternative Rock
  • Alternative Metal
  • Nu Metal

About the Song

"Tying in with 'Hero,' here's one about feeling like you're falling under from all the stresses of life. Even though you feel like everyone around you is trying to take your hope away from you, no one has the right to do that and you shouldn't allow those negative influences to weigh you down. Live what you believe and don't be afraid to stand up for your faith. The song is about the idea that every day you wake up, there's a war for our souls."
- John Cooper on New Release Today
